Being a GTAC STEM Coach

About the Job
What is a STEM Coach
STEM Coaches facilitate the learning journey of primary and secondary school students by inspiring them to careers in science through cutting-edge research technologies.
“This role is such a fun gig! You can work when it suits you and get out of the lab to explore science with young people. It’s the perfect job for a PhD student”
What does a STEM Coach do
  • Lead groups of 6 students in the classroom.
  • Help students understand and apply new scientific concepts through effective questioning and positive reinforcement.
  • Engage students in dialogue, offering insights into STEM research and your academic journey.
About GTAC

Inspiring the Next Generation of Scientists

Coaches act as role models for students to envision themselves as future scientists.
